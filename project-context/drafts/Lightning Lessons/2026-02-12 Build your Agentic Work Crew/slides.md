## Slide 5: The Three-Layer Framework

### Understanding What Makes Agentic AI Work

Think of agentic AI like building a house:

**Layer 1: The Foundation (Model)**
- The raw intelligence (GPT-5, Claude, Gemini)
- Like choosing quality materials
- **Important, but not enough**

**Layer 2: The Structure (Agent Orchestration)**
- How AI plans, executes, validates
- Like having a skilled contractor
- **This is where the magic happens**

**Layer 3: The Blueprint (Context Engineering)**
- Your project's rules, patterns, knowledge
- Like architectural plans
- **This is YOUR competitive advantage**

## Slide 6: Why Context Engineering Matters

**Without Context:**

**The Key Insight:**  
Context engineering is where YOU create value — it's project-specific, organization-specific, and can't be copied.

## Slide 16: How Nova Works

### The 4-Step Framework

**Step 1: Role Analysis**
- You describe your job
- Nova identifies your key work activities
- Maps to standardized work patterns

**Step 2: Task Mapping**
- Breaks down your work into tasks
- Identifies what you struggle with
- Finds opportunities for AI assistance

**Step 3: Crew Design**
- Nova suggests 3-7 AI "teammates"
- Each has a specialized role
- Clear boundaries and responsibilities

**Step 4: Implementation**
- Crew gets built in Cursor
- You test and refine
- Crew learns your preferences

**What Gets Created:**

```

├── .cursor/

│   └── agents/         # Your crew members
├── AGENTS.md           # Shared crew memory
├── STATUS.md           # Task board

```

**The Key Files:**
- Each crew member has their own file
- Rules define how they work
- Shared memory keeps context
- Everything is version controlled

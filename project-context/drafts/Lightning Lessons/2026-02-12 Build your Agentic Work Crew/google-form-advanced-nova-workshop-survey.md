## Analysis Framework

After collecting responses, analyze:

1. **Audience composition:** Which roles dominate? Tailor examples accordingly.
2. **Desired outcomes:** What's the #1 result? Lead with that in marketing.
3. **Challenges:** What's the biggest barrier? Address it upfront in workshop.
4. **AI maturity:** What have they tried? Adjust technical level accordingly.
5. **Format preference:** Majority vote = primary format; consider offering multiple.
6. **Price sensitivity:** Median willingness to pay = pricing strategy; "depends on value" = need strong value prop.
7. **Specific integrations:** Most requested = prioritize in curriculum.

---

## Next Steps After Survey

1. **If strong interest (>30 responses, >60% willing to pay $200+):**
   - Design workshop curriculum based on top outcomes/integrations
   - Set pricing based on median willingness to pay
   - Choose format based on majority preference
   - Create landing page and announce dates

2. **If moderate interest (15-30 responses, mixed pricing):**
   - Follow up with email to clarify value proposition
   - Consider offering a pilot/beta workshop at lower price
   - Gather more specific use cases

3. **If low interest (<15 responses, mostly "not sure"):**
   - Reassess if workshop is the right format
   - Consider free advanced tutorials or documentation instead
   - Focus on building Nova community first

## Usage Notes

### Personalization
- **Use their first name** if available in the contact list
- **If they clicked the Feb 15 email:** Add "I saw you clicked my email on Feb 15 — thanks for engaging" after the first sentence
- **If they have a LinkedIn profile:** Reference their role/company if relevant (e.g., "As a [role] at [company], this course would help you...")
- **If no name:** Use "Hi there," or skip greeting and start with "I noticed..."

### Channel Options
1. **Email (Maven personal message):** Use subject line above, send as personal message through Maven
2. **LinkedIn DM:** If you have their LinkedIn profile, send as a direct message (more personal, higher response rate)
3. **Email reply:** If they replied to the Feb 15 email, reply directly to that thread

### Prioritization Order
1. **First:** Contacts who clicked the Feb 15 email (7.4% click rate = 2–3 people) — highest intent
2. **Second:** Contacts with LinkedIn profiles + job titles visible — easier to personalize
3. **Third:** Remaining "Started payment" contacts

### Timing
- **Start today (Feb 17)** — don't wait until Feb 18–19
- **Send 1–2 per hour** to avoid looking automated
- **Follow up:** If no reply by Wed Feb 18, send a shorter "Last 48 hours" version

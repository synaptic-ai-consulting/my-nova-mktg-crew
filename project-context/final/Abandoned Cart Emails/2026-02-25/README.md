# Abandoned Cart Emails — Generic (Post–Cohort 3)

**Version date:** 2026-02-25  
**Use:** Default abandoned-enrollment sequence when no cohort deadline campaign is running.

## Why this version

- **2026-02-10** was the Cohort 3 conversion campaign: 5 emails (1hr, 6hr, 1day, 2day, 3day) with "Cohort 3 closes February 20" and last-chance urgency.
- **2026-02-03** was the pre-campaign generic set: 4 emails (1hr, 1day, 2day, 4day), value-led, no cohort/deadline copy.
- **2026-02-25** reverts to the 2026-02-03 structure and tone so the sequence stays generic until the next cohort launch.

## Sequence (4 emails)

| # | Lag  | Subject |
|---|------|--------|
| 1 | 1 hr | I'm holding your spot for you |
| 2 | 1 day | Still thinking about joining? |
| 3 | 2 days | Busy? That's exactly why this was built. |
| 4 | 4 days | Don't just take my word for it. |

## Conversion rationale

- **Email 1 (1hr):** Light touch, why-now (AI/agentic shift), offer to talk or finish enrolling.
- **Email 2 (1day):** Objection-handling (time, level, career) + clear value prop and CTA.
- **Email 3 (2day):** ICP + outcomes (personas, AAMAD, HITL, observability) + relevance to busy practitioners.
- **Email 4 (4day):** Market stats + social proof (reviews image) + final CTA.

No cohort or date-specific urgency; suitable for ongoing use and for switching back to a campaign-specific sequence when the next cohort opens.

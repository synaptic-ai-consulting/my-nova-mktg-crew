## Notes

- Send each email **individually**, not as a BCC batch. Each one should go to one person.
- Do NOT copy the Maven broadcast tone — no headers, no emojis, no bullet points. Plain text only.
- Keep the subject line short and personal-sounding. Avoid words like "enroll", "discount", "course" in the subject.
- If someone replies, that's the opening. The goal of this email is just to start a conversation — not to close on the first message.
- If you convert, use LL25 ($1,125) as your first offer. Only go to a custom code if they engage and push back on price.

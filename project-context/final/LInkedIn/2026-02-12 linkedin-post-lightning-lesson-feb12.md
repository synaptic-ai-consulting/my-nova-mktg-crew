## Post Copy

We're in the middle of a silent revolution.

Millions of us got our first taste of AI through chat — ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude. Ask a question, get an answer. Copy, paste, repeat.

That was Gen 1. Useful. But it's not the future.

Gen 2 is agentic. AI that doesn't just answer — it *does*. It reads your files, edits documents, pulls context, runs workflows. It works *with* you, not just *for* you in a chat bubble.

And here's the thing: it's no longer just for developers.

Anthropic's legal team cut marketing review turnaround from 2–3 days to 24 hours. Mark Pike, their Associate General Counsel, built workflows with Claude — and he doesn't code. He said it best: "The first time I saw Claude Code, it looked like something from the Matrix. But then I just typed a normal sentence, describing what I wanted. And it worked."

Claude Cowork launched this January. Same idea: turn Claude from a conversational assistant into a virtual coworker that plans, executes, and delivers completed work — not just text in a chat window.

The trend is clear: agentic productivity started with engineers. It's now expanding to every knowledge worker.

The challenge? Tools like Cursor — a powerhouse for agentic work — were built for developers. The UX can feel intimidating. But writers at Lenny's Newsletter put it simply: Cursor is just a file explorer + a text editor + an AI chat, all in one. No Matrix, no jargon. Just three things you already know how to use.

That's why I built Nova.

Nova is a work crew builder that helps non-technical people step into agentic productivity. You describe your role and what you need help with; Nova designs a small crew of AI specialists that work inside Cursor. No coding. You stay in control.

What you get:
→ Immediate access to the latest LLMs — no choosing, no switching  
→ A powerful agentic harness: tools, local knowledge, context — the layer that turns raw AI into a real workflow  
→ Your work lives on your machine, not in someone else's cloud  
→ You approve every step. Nothing runs without you.

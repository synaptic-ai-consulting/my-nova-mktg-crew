# Cohort 4 — LinkedIn vs Newsletter Strategy Recommendation

**For:** Riley (Acquisition & Audience Strategist)  
**Re:** LinkedIn post updates — Learning Roadmap Launch  
**Date:** 2026-03-03  
**Context:** Authority-building TOFU approach; Newsletter = Mar 4–5; LinkedIn = Mar 3

---

## Strategic Decision

**Approach:** LinkedIn = authority content only (tease); Newsletter = full announcement (Bronze/Silver/Gold).

This keeps LinkedIn pure thought leadership and positions the newsletter as the payoff — where subscribers get the full analysis *and* the Learning Roadmap announcement.

---

## Recommendations for Riley

### 1. LinkedIn Post — Remove Full Announcement

**Current draft** ends with:
- "And today I'm launching **the Agentic Architect Learning Roadmap** — three tiers to meet you exactly where you are."
- Full Bronze/Silver/Gold breakdown (🥉 🥈 🥇 with prices and dates)
- "Choose your path. Links in the comments."

**Change to:**
- End with the newsletter CTA only.
- Remove the explicit Learning Roadmap launch and tier breakdown from the post body.

**Suggested closing (replace the announcement block):**

> I wrote a full deep dive in my latest newsletter — why the industry is aligning, what the data shows, what the tools are doing, and what you can do about it. Link in the comments.

**Optional soft tease** (add one line before the newsletter CTA if you want to hint at the offer without listing tiers):

> In the newsletter I also share the Learning Roadmap — three paths for engineers at different stages.

---

### 2. Links in First Comment

**Primary CTA:** Newsletter link (full deep dive + announcement lives there).

**Optional:** Bronze (Learn AAMAD) link — low-friction entry for people who don't want to subscribe yet. Keeps a soft entry point without turning the post into a sales pitch.

**Remove from comment** (or deprioritize): Silver and Gold direct links. Let the newsletter be the conversion moment for paid tiers.

---

### 3. Newsletter — No Changes

Newsletter draft stays as-is: full authority deep dive + full Learning Roadmap announcement (Part 6: Choose Your Path) with Bronze/Silver/Gold CTAs.

---

## Rationale Summary

| Factor | Authority-only LinkedIn | Full announcement on both |
|--------|--------------------------|----------------------------|
| Channel clarity | LinkedIn = thought leadership; Newsletter = conversion | Both channels mix authority + sell |
| Subscriber quality | People who subscribe for the deep dive are warmer | Broader reach, colder leads |
| Engagement | Less "salesy" → better reach/algorithm | Can feel like an ad |
| Conversion path | Newsletter → announcement (one extra step) | Shorter path, but lower intent |

**Trade-off:** Slightly longer path to conversion, but warmer leads and stronger authority positioning.

## Executive Summary

This plan doubles down on what worked in Cohort 3 while adding a **content-led acquisition engine** fueled by the 50 authoritative sources on the Agentic Architect momentum. The strategy: **acquire more** (authority content + Lightning Lessons + LinkedIn), **nurture smarter** (segment by intent, 24–48h LL follow-up), and **convert harder** (1:1 outreach, scholarship, abandoned cart).

**Target:** 40 students (vs. 20 in Cohort 3)  
**Runway:** 35 days (vs. 12 days for C3 intensive campaign)  
**Core insight:** Everyone who responded to a 1:1 DM in C3 enrolled. The bottleneck is response rate — more warm leads + earlier outreach = more conversions.

**Portfolio expansion:** This campaign includes the **unified launch of the Agentic Architect Learning Roadmap** — all three tiers (Bronze, Silver, Gold) announced together with a single communication strategy. Silver Session 1 is ~Mar 23, so **launch must happen immediately** (Mar 3). See [Part 2A: Learning Roadmap Launch](#part-2a-learning-roadmap-launch-bronze--silver--gold) and [Part 2B: Waitlist Strategy](#part-2b-waitlist-strategy).

## Part 1: Cohort 3 Results & Learnings

### What Worked (Double Down)

| Lever | Performance | Action for C4 |
|-------|-------------|---------------|
| **1:1 DM / personal email** | Highest ROI — everyone who responded enrolled | Start earlier; prioritize "started payment" + LL attendees; batch weekly |
| **Scholarship ($750)** | Several high-intent conversions | Launch C4 scholarship by Mar 15; 10 seats; need-based positioning |
| **Abandoned cart sequence** | 1h: 62.5%, 6h: 66.7% open; 1d: 15.6% click | Keep generic sequence; add C4 deadline copy in final 2 weeks |
| **Extension broadcast** | 35% open; gave weekend to decide | Plan extension option for Apr 4–5 if near goal |
| **LL follow-up within 24–48h** | 40.3% open, 3.1% click — highest intent window | Non-negotiable: send within 24h of every LL |

### What Didn't Work (Fix for C4)

| Issue | Root Cause | Fix |
|-------|------------|-----|
| LL35 narrowcasts 0–1.9% click | Sent to signups, not attendees; 1+ week delay; discount-led | Segment by **attendance only**; send within 24–48h; lead with **content**, discount as footnote |
| Scholarship revival broadcast | 0.8% click; mentioned 3+ times | No standalone scholarship broadcast; include in combined urgency; 1:1 for applicants |
| LL promo fatigue | LL35 in 5+ emails | Keep LL35 **exclusive** — LL narrowcasts + follow-ups only, not every broadcast |

### Funnel Baseline (Feb 24)

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| LP visitors (cumulative) | 2,947 |
| Clicked Enroll | 135 |
| Visitor → Click | ~4.58% |
| Click → Purchase | ~23% |
| Waitlist | ~1,899 |
| C4 already enrolled (Feb 21) | 3 |

**Math for 40 enrollments:** At 4.5% visitor→click and 23% click→purchase, we need ~174 new clicks → ~3,870 new visitors. But we also convert from existing waitlist. Realistic split: **~15–20 from waitlist/LL nurture**, **~20–25 from net-new acquisition** (content + LL + LinkedIn).

*Full analysis: `project-context/research/maven-multi-course-waitlist-strategy.md`*

**Recommended approach: Lead Magnets + Lightning Lessons mapped to Gold**

**Implementation:** Ensure all LM and LL used in Bronze/Silver comms are mapped to Gold in Maven. Add these CTAs to Learn AAMAD page, Bronze post-completion, Silver LP, and Silver post-enrollment. Manual add = fallback for contacts who don't engage LM/LL.
